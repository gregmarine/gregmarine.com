# Ingredients

- 4 skinless chicken breast halves
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 3 tbsp brown sugar
- freshly ground salt and pepper
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp parsley
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp cumin

2.  Bake 20 minutes
